# NurseLink — PRD, Architecture & Launch Plan

> A modern, AI-powered social learning network for nursing students, aspiring nurses, and graduates preparing for the Philippine Nurse Licensure Exam (PNLE) and NCLEX.

---

## 1) Vision & Goals

* **Vision:** Build the first-ever *social learning world* for nurses — connecting aspiring students, current learners, and professional nurses through collaboration, competition, and compassion.
* **Primary Goals**

  1. Create a safe and interactive digital ecosystem that merges social media and nursing education.
  2. Inspire freshmen and future nursing students to join the community early and learn through real experiences.
  3. Support active nursing students with adaptive AI-powered study tools.
  4. Guide graduates toward licensure success and job opportunities.
  5. Build a global network where nurses learn, teach, and grow together.
  6. Enable monetization and sustainability through ethical, relevant advertising spaces.

## 3) Core Feature Overview

### 🩺 Learning System

* **AI Review Generator:** Creates adaptive quizzes, mocks, and assignments from topics (Y1–Y4 curriculum + PNLE/NCLEX blueprint).
* **Assignments:** Time-limited tasks with AI feedback and analytics.
* **Daily Challenge:** 24-hour competitive quiz for global and clan rankings.
* **Pre-Nursing Pathway:** Orientation and foundation modules for aspiring students.

### 🌐 CareSpace (Social Feed)

* Acts as the **Home Feed** of NurseLink.
* Users can post study tips, experiences, memes, motivational stories, and achievements.
* Like, comment, share, and follow system.
* **Trending Topics:** #MedSurg, #BoardJourney, #StudyTips, #FutureRN.
* **Mentorship Threads:** Connect freshmen with graduates and mentors.
* **Achievements Showcase:** Board passers, top scorers, and event highlights.

### 👥 Parties & Clans

* Create or join **Parties (2–10)** or **Clans (10–200)**.
* Clan Wars: Competitive quiz events with XP and rewards.
* Clan Rank & Honor Board: Based on total quiz performance and contribution.

### 🧠 AI Copilot — “NurseMind”

* Ask: “Make me a quiz from Med-Surg Cardiovascular.”
* Explain: “Rationale for question 12 like I’m a first-year.”
* Plan: “7-day review plan for weak areas in Pharma.”

### 🏫 Review Center & Job Hub

* **Review Center Posting:** ₱2,000/month per listing, visible for 40 days.

  * Show topnotchers, board passers, and international exam achievers.
  * Include schedules, links, and verified badges.
* **Job Hiring Page:** Hospitals and clinics post openings for:

  * Fresh graduates.
  * Board passers seeking experience.
  * Internship or volunteer programs.

### 💼 Advertisement Space Page

* **AdSpace Page:** A dedicated section where verified sponsors and educational partners can advertise.

  * Review centers, nursing schools, hospitals, and uniform suppliers can showcase promotions or events.
  * Pricing tiers: ₱1,000 basic (30 days) / ₱3,000 featured (60 days).
  * Ads appear in rotating banners within CareSpace and on the AdSpace Page.
  * **Ad Categories:** Education, Healthcare, Career Opportunities, Student Lifestyle, and Tools.
  * Admin moderation ensures all ads are educationally aligned and safe.
* **Ad Analytics Dashboard:** Shows impressions, clicks, and engagement.

### 📈 Progress & Gamification

* **XP, streaks, mastery charts.**
* **Badges:** Quiz Streaks, Top Clans, Consistent Learner.
* **Levels:** 1–50 ranks (Student → Nurse Leader).
