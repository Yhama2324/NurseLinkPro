# CKalingaLink — PRD, Architecture & Launch Plan

> A Filipino-built, AI-powered social learning world for aspiring nurses, students, and graduates — connecting care, courage, and community.

---

## 1) Vision & Goals

* **Vision:** Build the most compassionate and connected learning platform for nursing — merging education, mentorship, and social connection in one unified ecosystem.
* **Mission:** To empower the next generation of nurses through experience, not shortcuts — providing tools, mentorship, and a community built on *kalinga* (care).

### 🎯 Goals

1. Inspire students from pre-nursing to professional practice through interactive, experience-based learning.
2. Build a social platform (CareSpace) where nurses share stories, motivation, and support.
3. Offer AI-powered study tools to improve board exam readiness (PNLE & NCLEX).
4. Connect users to review centers, schools, hospitals, and job opportunities.
5. Establish a monetization system to sustain growth through ethical ads and partnerships.

## 3) Core Feature Overview

### 🩺 Learning System

* **AI Review Generator:** Builds adaptive quizzes, exams, and practice boards based on topics (Y1–Y4 + PNLE/NCLEX domains).
* **Assignments:** Custom and timed exercises with AI feedback and analytics.
* **Daily Challenge:** 24-hour event-style quizzes with leaderboard tracking.
* **Pre-Nursing Pathway:** Intro modules for aspiring students and freshmen.

### 🌐 CareSpace (Social Feed)

* Home feed where users post study tips, experiences, memes, and stories.
* Comment, like, share, and hashtag system (#StudyRN, #BoardJourney, #NurseGoals).
* Mentorship threads linking freshmen to senior students and professionals.
* Clan/Party posts, announcements, and study achievements.
* Moderated for positive and educational content.

### 👥 Parties & Clans

* **Parties:** 2–10 members for group studies.
* **Clans:** 10–200 members, competing in challenges and earning XP.
* **Clan Wars:** Timed competitions rewarding engagement and accuracy.

### 🧠 AI Copilot — “NurseMind”

* Generate quizzes from chosen topics.
* Explain rationales in conversational mode.
* Create personalized 7-day or 30-day study plans.
* Recommend practice sets and motivational reminders.

### 🏫 Review Center & Job Hub

* **Review Center Listing:** ₱2,000/month for 40 days; highlights achievements, topnotchers, and NCLEX passers.
* **Job Board:** Hospitals and clinics can post openings for board passers, fresh graduates, and interns.
* **Verification Badges:** for legitimate review centers and employers.

### 💼 AdSpace (Advertisement Page)

* Dedicated section for sponsored educational and healthcare partners.
* **Tiers:** ₱1,000 (Basic 30 days) / ₱3,000 (Featured 60 days).
* Ad categories: Education, Career, Tools, Uniforms, and Equipment.
* Ad analytics dashboard for impressions and engagement.
* Ads also appear as rotating banners inside CareSpace.

### 📈 Progress & Gamification

* **XP System:** Earn points for quizzes, posts, and challenges.
* **Badges:** Consistency, Accuracy, Top Scorer, Community Helper.
* **Ranks:** Student → Practitioner → Mentor → Leader.

## 7) Architecture Overview

* **Frontend:** React Native (mobile) + PWA (web).
* **Backend:** Node.js (NestJS) or Python (FastAPI).
* **Database:** PostgreSQL (core), Redis (cache), S3 (media).
* **AI Layer:** OpenAI API (quiz gen, rationale, tutoring).
* **Payments:** Stripe / Xendit / GCash integration.
* **Auth:** Firebase Auth / Google / Email login.
* **Analytics:** ClickHouse or BigQuery + Metabase.
* **Infra:** Railway / Vercel / Render deployment.
